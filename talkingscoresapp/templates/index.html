Talking Scores - making sheet music more accessible for blind and print impaired musicians

Transform your MusicXML files into detailed, spoken descriptions with synchronized audio playback. Designed for blind, visually impaired, and print impaired musicians, as well as anyone learning to read music or preferring auditory learning methods.
